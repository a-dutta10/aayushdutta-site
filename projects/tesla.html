OVERVIEW

During my time at Tesla, I worked on the design, packaging, and validation of wiring harness systems supporting automotive and energy products. My role emphasized translating system-level requirements into manufacturable, serviceable hardware while supporting rapid iteration in a production-driven environment.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Designed and modified wiring harness layouts to integrate electromechanical components
  • Supported component selection and validation for prototype and production builds
  • Authored build instructions and documentation for complex harness assemblies
  • Worked closely with fabrication teams to troubleshoot issues during assembly and bring-up
  • Assisted in debugging and quality checks across pre- and post-production stages

IMPACT & LEARNING

  • Gained hands-on experience designing hardware for high-volume, high-reliability systems
  • Learned to balance electrical, mechanical, and manufacturing constraints under tight timelines
  • Developed an appreciation for robust documentation and cross-functional communication
  • Strengthened understanding of how early design decisions affect downstream production and service
